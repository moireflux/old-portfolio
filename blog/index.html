            # Re:Site 04.06.2020

            Due to mysterious circumstances this website broke down. The Problem hasn't been found and will be willingly kept lost. But it led us to the decision, to do a cleaner hosting flow. This also included a new domain-name, for the little expenses of around 3$ (us-dollar) per year. Price mostly depends on the ending (.net; .com; .info; .digital; etc.) and the shorter and the more easy words are also get quite expensive. Nevertheless the website is online and working so far. Next goals: working on the theme, adding more pictures and infos.
